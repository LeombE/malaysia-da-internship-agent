## Methodology

The pipeline collects job data from OpenWeb Ninja JSearch API and optional responsible sources such as Adzuna API, Google Alerts RSS, and public company career pages. It normalizes job records into a shared `JobPosting` model, scores each role with configurable business rules, stores history in SQLite, deduplicates exported rows, and publishes CSV outputs.

Scoring considers:

- Role relevance.
- Internship/date/duration fit.
- Location priority.
- Company tier.
- Source quality.
- Student/resume fit.
- Posting freshness.
- Risk penalties.

GitHub Actions automates the process 4 times per day during the 15-day urgent campaign, with a target of 160 JSearch requests per run.
